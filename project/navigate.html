On The Ground Guide

This section of the website provides info about food, transportationt accomodation and generally how to nagivate you way onces you arrive in Ghana

Transportation

The major means of transport is the trotro(small vans) for short travels and bussesfor long distance journeys. You may consider booking a flight for long distance travels within the country.

Services like uber, bolt operate well withink the cities but aren't as effect in small communities and rural areas

Food

Food in Ghana typically varies depending on what part or region of the country you find youself. However, the most common are rice, banku, fufuo. Other foods may include bayere ampesi, kokontealso known as abetie

Accomodation

There are hotels and guest houses. Google map works very well in the country so you could look up a few of them.
